ESCAPE CHARS

Chars to Escape
  1. Less than: < (USE &lt;)
  2. Greater than: > (USE &gt;)
  3. Ampersand: & (USE &amp;)
Other Cool Things
  • Non Breaking Space: (USE &nbsp;) ==> what this does is ensure words stay on the same line. Put between the words
  • Example: Random random random random random random random random random random random random random Keep on the same line
  • Can use (&quot) at start and end of text to wrap sentence in quotes;
